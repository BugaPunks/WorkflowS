## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Peer Review System
The system SHALL provide peer review functionality as specified in "Ideas Adicionales".

#### Scenario: Create peer review session
- **WHEN** a sprint or project concludes
- **THEN** the docente can initiate a peer review session
- **AND** students are automatically assigned to review their teammates
- **AND** review criteria align with project goals and rubrics
- **AND** system ensures balanced review distribution

#### Scenario: Submit peer review
- **WHEN** a student participates in peer review
- **THEN** they can provide anonymous feedback for each team member
- **AND** they rate specific aspects (collaboration, quality, timeliness)
- **AND** they can add qualitative comments and examples
- **AND** submission is confidential and only visible to docente

#### Scenario: View peer review insights
- **WHEN** a docente reviews peer feedback
- **THEN** they see aggregated feedback for each student
- **AND** they can identify patterns and consensus among reviewers
- **AND** they can use peer feedback as input for individual grades
- **AND** they can moderate inappropriate or biased feedback

#### Scenario: Peer review analytics
- **WHEN** analyzing team dynamics
- **THEN** docentes can view peer review trends over time
- **AND** they can identify students who consistently receive high ratings
- **AND** they can spot potential conflicts or collaboration issues
- **AND** they can compare self-assessment vs peer assessment patterns
